About VicFalls Televivi

The story behind the voice of Victoria Falls

The Voice of Victoria Falls

VicFalls Televivi is the official video channel of Victoria Falls Broadcasting & Media Production Company, delivering news, sports, culture and tourism stories from Victoria Falls and surrounding regions.

Our channel captures real community moments from Mosi Oa Tunya High School, Mapopoma Festival performances, Zambezi Zone Athletics, and the daily stories that shape life in Victoria Falls.

We publish in-depth video reports, live event coverage, and feature documentaries that connect residents, visitors and stakeholders across Zimbabwe.
