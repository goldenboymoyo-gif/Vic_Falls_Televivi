The story behind VicFalls Televivi

The Voice of Victoria Falls

VicFalls Televivi is a digital media platform dedicated to delivering trusted news, sports, entertainment, tourism and community stories from Victoria Falls and beyond. Through quality journalism and engaging storytelling, we strive to inform, educate and connect communities.

We are committed to providing timely coverage of events and creating a platform that showcases the people, culture and opportunities of Victoria Falls to Zimbabwe, Africa and the world.
